# Glossary

Quick reference for crafting terms used throughout these docs.

**Annul** — Remove 1 random modifier from an item using an Orb of Annulment.

**Blocker** — A cheap, common mod deliberately placed on an item to prevent that mod group from appearing on future rolls, and to improve fracture odds.

**Brick** — When a crafting step goes irreversibly wrong — typically locking the wrong mod via fracture, or destroying a key mod via annulment.

**Cross-slot** — PoE2's Exalted Orb draws from both prefix and suffix pools simultaneously, unlike PoE1 where the slot type was chosen first.

**Desecrate** — Apply an Abyssal Bone to add a hidden modifier, then reveal options at the Well of Souls. See [Desecration & Lich Mods](/crafting/desecration).

**Erasure** — Omen of Dextral/Sinistral Erasure. Forces the next Chaos Orb to remove a mod from one specific side only.

**Expected Value (EV)** — The average cost of a crafting step accounting for probability. EV = expected attempts × cost per attempt.

**Fracture** — Permanently lock 1 random mod on a Rare item using a Fracturing Orb. The locked mod cannot be removed or changed by any crafting currency.

**Item Level (ilvl)** — The item's level, which determines the highest-tier mods that can appear.

**Lich Mod** — A modifier exclusive to desecration, obtainable only through the Well of Souls. Tied to one of three Liches: Amanamu, Kurgal, or Ulaman.

**Lock** — See Fracture. A fractured mod is "locked."

**Min Modifier Level** — The minimum required level a mod must have to appear in the pool when using Greater or Perfect currencies. Filters out low-tier mods.

**Mod Level** — The required level of a specific modifier. Higher-tier mods have higher mod levels.

**Monte Carlo** — A simulation method. The advisor runs 10,000 simulated crafts to calculate realistic cost distributions. See [Cost Optimizer](/crafting/cost-optimizer).

**Omen** — A consumable that modifies the behavior of the next crafting currency used. See [Omens](/crafting/omens).

**Percentile (P25/P50/P75/P90)** — Budget thresholds from Monte Carlo simulation. P50 = median cost, P90 = 90th percentile (only 10% of crafters spend more).

**Pool** — The set of eligible mods that can roll on an item, filtered by item class, slot type, existing mods, and min modifier level.

**Prefix** — A modifier that occupies a prefix slot. Items can have up to 1 prefix (Magic) or 3 prefixes (Rare).

**Side** — Either the prefix side or suffix side of an item. The [side-first strategy](/crafting/side-first-strategy) builds one side completely before the other.

**Slam** — Informal term for using an Exalted Orb to add a modifier.

**Soft Risk** — A crafting step where failure can be retried without losing progress (e.g., scour + re-transmute).

**Suffix** — A modifier that occupies a suffix slot. Items can have up to 1 suffix (Magic) or 3 suffixes (Rare).

**Survival Rate** — The probability that an item makes it through the entire recipe without bricking. Product of all success probabilities.

**Weight** — A spawn weight assigned to each mod by GGG. Heavier mods are more likely to roll. Probability = weight ÷ total pool weight.

**Well of Souls** — The reveal mechanic for desecrated items. Presents 3 (or 6 with Abyssal Echoes) mod options to choose from.

**Whittling** — Omen of Whittling. Forces the next Chaos Orb to remove the mod with the globally lowest mod-level on the item.
